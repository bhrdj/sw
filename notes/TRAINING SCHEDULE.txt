TRAINING SCHEDULE

A newly employed caregiver in Everything is Learning daycares is expected to undergo training during the first month of employment. By the end of one month, he/she should be competent in the following areas.


Filling in attendance and safety forms
Interpreting and following Day care daily schedule
Watching EL videos
EL Doman book practices 
EL Day Care Manual
Carrying out conversations with the children.
Recording conversations held with children
Flashcard writing
Following interest
One on one EL sessions.
Reading material preparation
 Day care inventory ledgers
