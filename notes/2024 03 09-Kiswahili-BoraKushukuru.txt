BORA KUSHUKURU = better to give thanks

Bora hata nimshukuru Mungu, kwa hivi nilivyo
   It's even better that I thank God
   nilivyo = how I am: Hutaenda ofisini jinsi ulivyo kwa kinyasa? (short pants).
   In this way the way I am
   Muundo huu unajumuisha mahali peke yake: [nilipo, niliko, nilimo]
Maana hata kuna wengine hawajafika nilipo
Bora hata nimshukuru Mungu kwa hiki nilichopata
Maana hata kuna wengine wamekosa kabisa

Tabasamu njoo, nataka nibadili
   badili(T) = to change
Wangu mtazamo niwaze kitajiri
   mtazamo = vision
   tazama(T) = to watch: Ninatazama runinga. (TV)
Na wewe maumivu njoo nataka nibadili
   maumivu = pain
Wangu msimamo, niishi kijasiri
   msimamo = standing posture

Ile habari ya kulialia, kila siku nakataa

Na kama nilisainiwa, mkataba nafuta
   nilisainiwa = I was signed
   mkataba = agreement
   maktaba = library
   futa(T) = to erase, to renege on an agreement
Ndugu tuliowasaidia, wakaficha makucha
   ficha(T) = to hide
   makucha = claws
Wao walipo fanikiwa, wakataka kutushusha
   fanikiwa = to succeed
   shusha(T) = to lower: Mama alishushwa cheo kazini.
   cheo/vyeo = position/level/station of worker in workplace
Nafuta zile <why me>

Na nilipo, huenda kuna wengine wanapatamani
   huenda = huenda hivyo = it seems like (the way things usually go)
   tamani = desire
   wanatamani = people with desire
   wana*pa*tamana = people that desire that place (the place that I am) 
Zamani huko, nilishakataliwa na watu wa nyumbani
   nilishakataliwa ~ kataa
Kila kidogo, ninacholalamikia lakini nimepata
   lalamika(T) = to complain
   the little thing I've been complaining about (lacking), but I already got it
Wengine huko, wamepambana wamekosa kabisa
   pamba = cotton  (irrelevant)
   pambana(T) = jaribu(T) sana ingawa ni changamoto ngumu 
   mashindano ya kukula

Kumbe ni bora, kuridhika na hiki

viungo = spices
