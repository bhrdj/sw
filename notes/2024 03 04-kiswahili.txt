Nishike mkono ndugu, niweze kufika mbali
   sonorant = sauti ulimi unaguza ufizi
   dental = 
   sonorant before voiced dental (D) -> 1 syllable (mdudu, ndugu)          ********
   sonorant before unvoiced dental (T) -> 2 syllables (mtu)
   sonorant before voiced palatal (G) -> 2 syllables (mguu, ngamia)
   sonorant before voiced palatal with apostrophe -> 1 syllable (ng'ombe)  ********
   sonorant before unvoiced palatal -> 2 syllables (mkono)
   sonorant before voiced plosive -> 1 syllables (mbona, mbaya) (no N+B)   ********
   sonorant before unvoiced plosive -> 2 syllables (mpaka)
   sonorant before voiced fricative (Z) -> 2 syllables (mzizi, nzuri)
   sonorant before unvoiced fricative (S/SH) -> 2 syllables (msafiri, mshahara)

Barua ya ruhusa ya kazi inachukua nafasi ya barua ya ruhusa ya mwanafunzi.

Ruhusa ya mwanafunzi haianzi saa ya hesabu miezi na miaka kwa kupata uraia ama makazi ya nchi ya Kenya.

Sasa hivyo, maneno "kazi" na "mkazi" yanamaanisha mawazo tofauti. Nilisoma na mwalimu, nomino "mkazi" ilitoka kwa kitenzi "kaa". Sasa, kazi inamaanisha kufanya shuguli za kupata pesa, na mkazi inamaanisha mtu anayeishi mahali fulani. Lakini, ninadhahania labda nyakati zile, neno kazi ilitoka wazo la wakazi walihitaji kufanya mambo fulani kufaa kukaa pahali pake.

sasa, hivyo = the way things are [tunaweza kuweka pumziko]
sasa = now

Nyakati = period
